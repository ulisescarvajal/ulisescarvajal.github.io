Hobby vs Interest

obbies and interests are not the same thing. While the former is an activity in which people spend time to get relax, on the latter, people invest time on something that is interesting for them; to satisfy curiosity. According to the web site indeed.com “A hobby is an activity you may complete in your free time that brings you pleasure” and “An interest is a desire or need to learn more about a specific subject” (indeed.com, 2021. Extracted March 31, 2021).
